## 📊 Overview

This project is a complete **Retail Sales & Profit Analysis** on the popular **Superstore** dataset. It explores sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and seasonal trends through interactive Plotly visualizations.

The analysis answers critical business questions like:
- Which months generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which product categories and sub-categories are most/least profitable?
- How do different customer segments perform?
- What is the sales-to-profit ratio across segments?

Perfect for data science portfolios, business intelligence projects, or anyone learning EDA with real-world retail data.

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Name**: Sample - Superstore.csv
- **Source**: Classic Tableau Superstore dataset
- **Rows**: 9,994 orders
- **Columns**: 21 (Order Date, Ship Date, Customer Segment, Category, Sub-Category, Sales, Profit, Discount, Region, etc.)
- **Time Period**: 2016–2017

> **Note**: The dataset is included in the repository (`data/Sample - Superstore.csv`).

## ✨ Key Features & Analyses

- Data cleaning & preprocessing (date conversion, feature extraction)
- Monthly Sales & Profit trend analysis
- Category-wise and Sub-Category-wise performance
- Customer Segment analysis (Sales vs Profit)
- Sales-to-Profit Ratio calculation
- Interactive Plotly visualizations (line charts, pie charts, bar charts)

## 📈 Visualizations Included

| Visualization                        | Chart Type      | Insight Highlight                  |
|--------------------------------------|-----------------|------------------------------------|
| Monthly Sales Trend                  | Line Chart      | Peak in November & December        |
| Monthly Profit Trend                 | Line Chart      | Strongest growth in Q4             |
| Sales by Category                    | (Grouped)       | Technology leads                   |
| Profit by Category                   | Pie Chart       | Technology = 52% of total profit   |
| Profit by Sub-Category               | Bar Chart       | Copiers & Phones most profitable   |
| Sales & Profit by Customer Segment   | Grouped Bar     | Consumer segment dominates         |

All charts are **fully interactive** (hover, zoom, export).
